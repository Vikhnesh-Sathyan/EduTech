Task 3: Music Theory Basics

Learn the fundamental concepts of music theory that will help you in your composition.

Scales

A scale is a series of notes in a specific order. The most common scale is the major scale.

  • Example: C Major Scale - C, D, E, F, G, A, B

Intervals

An interval is the distance between two notes. Here are some common intervals:

  • Major Second: 2 half steps (C to D)
  • Perfect Fifth: 7 half steps (C to G)
  • Minor Sixth: 8 half steps (C to Ab)

Key Signatures

Key signatures indicate the key of the piece of music. For example:

  • G Major: 1 sharp (F#)
  • D Major: 2 sharps (F# and C#)
